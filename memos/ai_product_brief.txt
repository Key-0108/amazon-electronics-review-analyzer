EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Analysis of 104,986 customer reviews reveals a solid average rating of 4.08/5, but an 18.2% negative review rate (19,070 reviews) signals meaningful quality and reliability gaps that warrant immediate attention. Complaint patterns point to systemic product integrity issues rather than isolated incidents, suggesting upstream supplier and QC failures.

TOP 3 CRITICAL ISSUES
1. **Device & connectivity issues (32% of complaints)** — The single largest driver of dissatisfaction, affecting an estimated 6,056 customers; persistent connectivity failures erode trust and elevate return rates and support costs.
2. **Defective on arrival (17% of complaints)** — Nearly 1-in-6 negative reviewers received non-functional products, directly damaging brand perception and inflating reverse logistics expenses.
3. **Audio & sound quality (15% of complaints)** — Underperforming core functionality suggests specification misrepresentation or manufacturing inconsistency, compounding the "doesn't work as advertised" complaints (11%) into a compounding credibility risk.

RECOMMENDED ACTIONS
1. **Audit top-complaint SKUs immediately** — Cross-reference complaint themes against specific product listings to identify high-risk items; prioritize suspension or delisting of units with elevated DOA and connectivity failure rates.
2. **Strengthen pre-shipment QC standards** — Work with suppliers to implement mandatory functional testing protocols for connectivity and audio performance before inventory is accepted into fulfillment.
3. **Enhance product listing accuracy** — Review advertised specifications against verified performance benchmarks and update listings to reduce "doesn't work as advertised" and accessories compatibility complaints.

RISK IF UNADDRESSED
Continued erosion in product reliability perception risks accelerating negative review velocity, suppressing conversion rates, and triggering increased return and chargeback costs. At scale, this threatens category reputation and could drive high-value customers to competing platforms.
